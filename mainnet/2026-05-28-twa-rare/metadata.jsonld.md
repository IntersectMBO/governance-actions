## Title

Rare Evo and Dev Gov Day 2026: Cardano Title Sponsorship

## Abstract

The primary mission of this proposal is to strengthen and expand Cardano's global ecosystem presence through Rare Evo 2026 and Rare Dev Gov Day 2026, two established community-led initiatives that have become major coordination, onboarding, networking, governance, and industry outreach platforms for the Cardano ecosystem. 

**This proposal would position the Cardano ecosystem as the title sponsor of the Rare Evo 2026 main stage and global livestream, while also supporting the second annual Rare Dev Gov Day, a dedicated Cardano-focused technical, governance, and community coordination event occurring ahead of Rare Evo on July 28th, 2026 at ARIA Las Vegas.** 

The proposal is designed to create direct ecosystem value through: 

- Global Cardano brand visibility 
- Ecosystem onboarding and education 
- Governance participation 
- Developer and startup exposure 
- Enterprise and institutional outreach 
- Cross-chain industry engagement 
- Community retention 
- Expanded accessibility for Cardano ecosystem participants 
- Rare Network will return 20% of all VIP Ticket sales back to the Cardano Treasury (current retail value of a VIP ticket is $900) 

During a market contraction period many ecosystem projects face reduced marketing, travel, and business development budgets following the slowdown of traditional ecosystem funding sources and general macroeconomics impacts. Maintaining strong ecosystem visibility, participation, and coordination infrastructure during difficult market cycles is critical for long-term ecosystem resilience and growth. 

The proposal is not intended to create a new initiative from scratch, but rather to expand and strengthen an already proven platform with a multi-year track record of execution and ecosystem impact. Rare Evo 2026 will occur regardless of treasury funding; however, this proposal allows the Cardano ecosystem to substantially expand its presence, accessibility, participation, and strategic industry outreach through a highly leveraged and already operational event infrastructure. 

Rare Network is requesting ₳2,750,000 based on a $0.24 value for $660,000 budget from the Cardano treasury. This will cover two sponsorship packages for Cardano. One sponsorship for Rare Evo 2026 as the title sponsorship and a second title sponsorship for Cardano Dev Gov Day 2026.

## Motivation

Rare Network has been active in the Cardano ecosystem since 2020, beginning with the launch of the RARE stake pool and evolving into one of the most consistent producers of Cardano-focused community building and ecosystem growth events, activations, and ecosystem media. Over the past half decade, the organization has built extensive operational infrastructure, industry relationships, and community trust through the successful execution of conferences, governance initiatives, side events, ecosystem showcases, and global blockchain activations. 

The team is the creator and operator of Rare Evo, one of the largest independently operated Cardano community-led conferences, held annually for five consecutive years. What began as a grassroots ecosystem initiative has grown into a major North American blockchain conference that brings together developers, founders, enterprises, investors, governance participants, creators, media, and community members from across the digital asset industry. 

Rare Evo has consistently provided a highly visible platform for Cardano ecosystem participation while simultaneously introducing external blockchain communities, enterprises, and developers to Cardano technology, governance, and ecosystem projects within a broader multi-chain environment. 

The conference has hosted major Cardano ecosystem participants, including leadership from organizations such as PENTAD, while also creating accessible opportunities for startups and community projects through discounted or complimentary booths, sponsorships, speaking opportunities, workshops, and ecosystem networking activations. Recent editions of Rare Evo have featured more than 70 exhibitor booths, multiple stages, livestream production, technical workshops, industry panels, networking experiences, and large-scale community activations. 

In 2025, the team launched the first Cardano Dev Gov Day, a dedicated Cardanofocused technical and governance event held ahead of Rare Evo. The event was intentionally designed as a free and publicly accessible community coordination platform with no requirement to hold a Rare Evo conference ticket. Cardano Dev Gov Day successfully brought together developers, governance contributors, ecosystem organizations, and community members for a full day of workshops, panels, networking, and ecosystem collaboration. The inaugural event welcomed approximately 500 attendees and featured a keynote from Charles Hoskinson alongside governance discussions, technical education, ecosystem workshops, and community-building activities. 

Beyond Rare Evo itself, the organization has maintained active involvement in Cardano governance and ecosystem growth initiatives. Team members participated in the formation of Intersect's Growth & Marketing Committee and have contributed to governance workshops and ecosystem coordination efforts internationally, including activations across North America, Europe, and Asia. As a formal contributor to Cardano's Unified Global Events Marketing Strategy, the team has also produced Cardano ecosystem booths, networking events, and side activations at major global blockchain conferences year-round. 

Additional ecosystem initiatives have included the pilot launch of Amplify Cardano, a community-led open ecosystem marketing and events fund. Operationally, the team successfully coordinated the global Project Catalyst Fund 12 launch campaign, which included 15 in-person events and 12 virtual activations worldwide. Additional community support efforts have included sponsorship and collaboration with independent ecosystem events such as GM Cardano and other grassroots community-building initiatives. 

Collectively, the organization has produced more than 60 blockchain industry side events and activations at conferences including ETHDenver, TOKEN2049, Consensus, Duke Blockchain Conference, Paris Blockchain Week, Cardano Summit, and more. 

A key differentiator behind these efforts is the existing operational infrastructure, venue relationships, sponsor network, production capabilities, volunteer coordination, and industry partnerships already established over multiple years of execution. This existing infrastructure significantly reduces execution risk while enabling ecosystem initiatives to operate at a scale and professionalism that would otherwise require substantially greater resources to replicate from scratch.

## **Contract Management**

A written off-chain Legal Contract will be created between Rare Network and the Cardano Development Holdings (CDH), as mandated by the constitution, and will be administered by Intersect. This will include details of the project delivery schedule and dispute resolution.

## **Project Delivery**

All milestones, acceptance criteria, payment amounts and expected delivery dates will be agreed between Rare Network and Intersect, acting on behalf of the CDH. Rare Network will deliver according to the agreed-upon project schedule within the Legal Contract, of which the necessary information will be made public via the budget management platform via transaction metadata. Defined by the milestones within a Legal Contract, Rare Network will submit and attest milestone acceptance to the community, Intersect or 3rd Party Assurer. Project progress will be monitored via Intersect's delivery assurance function which will be communicated to the community. Acceptance of the above work is expected to be supported by a 3rd Party Assurer, who will be responsible for reviewing and signing off the work completed at each project milestone against the corresponding milestone deliverables detailed within the Legal Contract.

## **Budget Management Tooling** 

To administrate treasury funds on-chain, Intersect will utilize the treasury management smart contract framework developed by Sundae Labs. The smart contracts have been extensively tested including audits from TxPipe and MLabs. Intersect will utilize a single Treasury Reserve Smart Contract (TRSC), with many Project-Specific Smart Contracts (PSSC), managed by Intersect. Intersect's management consists of three 'admin' and two Intersect 'leadership' roles. An Oversight Committee consisting of five external, independent third-party entities (Sundae Labs, Cardano Foundation, Dquadrant, Xerberus and NMKR) will provide checks and balances on Intersect and safeguard against errors and unilateral control. Full details on Intersect's smart contract configuration are available in the Smart Contract Guide on their knowledge base.
