## Title

Cardano at TOKEN2049 Singapore 2026: Baseline ‘Platinum' Sponsorship Proposal

## Abstract

This proposal seeks to catapult the Cardano ecosystem into the global spotlight at TOKEN2049 Singapore, the world’s premier crypto event and the ultimate nexus for industry amplification. The proposal requests **3,303,750 ada** (equivalent to **$792,900 USD**, calculated at an estimated rate of $0.24 per ada) to establish a high-impact, community-centric presence that serves as a dedicated stage for Cardano’s builders.

A strategic takeover of Asia's most influential blockchain hub. By securing the second largest booth and a dedicated Cardano stage, we're giving builders something no other ecosystem offers at this scale. Free conference tickets, a stage to present, undeniable access to 25,000+ decision makers, high-value networking, and post-event photography and video coverage, all within the ‘Platinum’ sponsorship. Built by the community, for the builders, ensuring Cardano's technical superiority is matched by an unmissable physical presence. Putting Cardano builders at the forefront.

The decision to present this as an independent proposal reflects EMURGO's direct response to community feedback. Following the submission of the combined Cardano Summit 2026 and TOKEN2049 proposal, DReps and community members raised legitimate concerns about the scale of the budget. In response, we have restructured the proposal, lowering the sponsorship from ‘Title’ to ‘Platinum’. This change reduces the overall budget while preserving the core strategic value of the sponsorship, demonstrating a collaborative approach to treasury management.

EMURGO, headquartered in Singapore and holding a multi-year partnership with TOKEN2049 spanning 2022 through 2025, is very well-positioned to execute this sponsorship with maximum efficiency and impact.

## Motivation

### Empowering Builders on the Global Stage

TOKEN2049 Singapore represents the most significant opportunity of the year to showcase the vibrancy of the Cardano ecosystem. The core motivation of this proposal is to provide Cardano builders with a professional, high-traffic platform to demonstrate their innovations. We recognize that for many projects, the barrier to entry for major international conferences is high; therefore, this proposal specifically includes the provision of event tickets and dedicated showcase space for ecosystem builders, ensuring that the people actually growing the network are the ones representing it.

By planting the Cardano flag in Singapore, the heart of the global Web3 economy, we create a "force multiplier" effect. Our presence will facilitate high-level networking, drive project adoption, and demonstrate to the broader crypto world that Cardano is a thriving, builder-focused ecosystem ready for mass-scale integration.

### The Role of EMURGO as Facilitator

To ensure the professional execution of this large-scale endeavor, EMURGO will serve as the operational facilitator. This role is uniquely suited to EMURGO for three key reasons:

1. **Local Expertise:** Headquartered in Singapore, EMURGO possesses the local network, vendor relationships, and logistical "know-how" required to navigate this complex environment efficiently.

2. **Proven Track Record:** With years of experience managing large-scale activations at global events, including previous iterations of TOKEN2049, EMURGO brings the institutional reliability needed to execute a world-class presence.

3. **Direct Pass-Through Funding:** EMURGO’s involvement is strictly to enable this community opportunity. All requested funds are designated as a direct pass-through to cover the hard costs of the venue, event delivery, build-out, and tickets. This ensures that 100% of the community’s investment is transformed into tangible value and visibility for Cardano builders.

### Benefits to the Cardano Community

This activation is a community-first initiative designed to deliver direct, traceable value to the Cardano ecosystem. By securing a flagship presence at TOKEN2049 Singapore, we are not just buying visibility; we are building a strategic platform for our ecosystem’s most important asset: its builders.

The primary focus of this proposal is to provide a world-class stage for Cardano projects. Key benefits for builders include:

**Provision of Event Tickets:** We will provide up to 30 dedicated conference tickets for Cardano builders, ensuring they are on the ground at Marina Bay Sands to represent their projects.

**Platform & Presence:** Builders will have access to a dedicated builder-stage within the Cardano flagship booth and a curated exhibition space to demonstrate live product updates and production-ready applications.

**Global Networking:** We will facilitate direct introductions between our ecosystem’s builders and a concentrated audience of 25,000+ institutional investors, enterprise decision-makers, and industry leaders.

**Long-Term Content Assets:** Every presentation will be professionally captured by an exclusive videographer, with raw and edited footage provided to projects to amplify their own marketing efforts long after the event ends.

‘Platinum’ Sponsorship includes Cardano-branded Wi-Fi and the inclusion of the Cardano booklet into each welcome bag for all attendees, intending to showcase the logos of each project.

What this proposal guarantees is that every Cardano builder who can attend TOKEN2049 will be given a premier position at the largest Web3 event in the world — substage slot, booth presence, and curated introductions. The activation is structured as a coordinated, pass-through platform, aggregating resources to unlock premium positioning, substage access, and dedicated on-site support at a scale that is typically inefficient for individual projects to secure on their own.

### Accountability

#### Administration of Funds

All critical phases, acceptance benchmarks, funding disbursements, and projected timelines will be established through a legal agreement between EMURGO and the Cardano Development Holdings (CDH), as mandated by the Constitution, and will be administered by Intersect. EMURGO is committed to fulfilling the project schedule as outlined in the formal legal agreement. Essential details of this arrangement will be transparently shared through the budget management system using transaction metadata.

As dictated by the milestones within the legal contract, EMURGO will present and certify the completion of each phase to the broader community, Intersect, or third-party assurer. The ongoing progress of the initiative will be closely tracked by Intersect's delivery assurance team, ensuring regular updates to the community.

The formal acceptance of completed work will be facilitated by a third-party assurer. This entity is tasked with evaluating and approving the deliverables at each project milestone, ensuring they meet the specific criteria set forth in the legal contract. The compensation for this assurance work is drawn from a designated portion of the treasury allocation.

#### Budget Administration Infrastructure

To securely manage treasury assets on-chain, Intersect will deploy the treasury management smart contract architecture created by Sundae Labs. These smart contracts have undergone [rigorous testing](https://github.com/SundaeSwap-finance/treasury-contracts/tree/main/offchain/tests), including comprehensive security audits conducted by TxPipe and MLabs. The conclusive mainnet validation test is publicly verifiable through the disburse action in transaction **0f591dc544ae14102dbb4a74d5311a6acffc1772b163d8b7a9656b9525950b17.**

This particular funding withdrawal will leverage Intersect’s 2025 treasury reserve contract, located at the address **stake17xzc8pt7fgf0lc0x7eq6z7z6puhsxmzktna7dluahrj6g6ghh5qjr.** It is planned that these funds will be transitioned to a new 2026 treasury reserve contract once it is fully operational.

#### Specifics of Budget Management

Intersect will employ a single Treasury Reserve Smart Contract (TRSC) alongside multiple Project-Specific Smart Contracts (PSSC), all under Intersect's administration. The governance structure within Intersect involves three administrative roles and two leadership roles. To ensure accountability, an Oversight Committee composed of five independent, external organizations will implement a system of checks and balances. This committee is designed to prevent unilateral decision-making and mitigate potential errors. While Intersect handles the day-to-day administration of both the TRSC and PSSCs, the Oversight Committee provides crucial external review for specific, high-impact actions.

The 2025 TRSC Oversight Committee includes Sundae Labs, the Cardano Foundation, Dquadrant, Xerberus, and NMKR. Their primary function is to objectively validate significant administrative operations using on-chain logic. They ensure that actions are accurate and consistent with established rules, without imposing subjective judgment on governance matters. Comprehensive details regarding Intersect's setup can be found in the [Smart Contract Guide](https://docs.intersectmbo.org/intersect-knowledge-base/archive/funding-administration-archive/intersect-administration-services/smart-contracts-as-part-of-our-administration) available in the knowledge base.

The top-level permissions are outlined below:

* TRSC Fund and PSSC Modify  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins, two of the five trusted entities, and one of the two Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Disperse  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins, three of the five trusted entities, and both Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Pause and Resume  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins and one of the two Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Sweep  
  * Authorization requires at least one of the three Intersect admins and one of the two Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Reorganize  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins and three of the five trusted entities to sign off.

#### Operational Procedures

Once this governance proposal is approved, project funds will be deposited into the TRSC's stake account. Neither the TRSC nor the PSSCs will be staked with a Stake Pool Operator (SPO); both will be delegated to the auto-abstain DRep. The funds will then be held in a UTxO within the TRSC.

When the 2026 TRSC is ready, funds will be migrated over via the disburse function. Once the legal contract is in place, funds will be transferred to a PSSC through the fund action, with all milestones and deliverables recorded in the transaction metadata.

A public dashboard will be made available for the community to audit the TRSC and PSSCs, track key metrics on the withdrawn ada, and verify all fund movements on-chain.

### Risks and Mitigation

Ada price volatility is the primary financial risk. A conservative rate of $0.24 per ada has been used. If the price drops significantly, EMURGO will scale back booth build and logistics while keeping the core sponsorship package intact. If the price rises, any surplus will be returned to the Cardano Treasury within six months of the event.

The risk of TOKEN2049 being cancelled is low given its strong track record and confirmed dates of October 7–8, 2026. Should a cancellation occur outside of EMURGO's control, all unspent funds, minus any unavoidable vendor commitments, will be returned to the Treasury along with a full cost breakdown.

Geopolitical disruptions or travel restrictions affecting Singapore could impact the event's value and level of attendance. If this occurs, EMURGO will assess the situation and either proceed at a reduced scale, postpone the activation, or return remaining funds to the Treasury. All decisions will be communicated transparently through the public dashboard.
