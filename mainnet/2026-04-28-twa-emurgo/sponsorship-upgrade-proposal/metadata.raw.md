## Title

Cardano at TOKEN2049 Singapore 2026: Top-Up ‘Title’ Sponsorship Upgrade

## Abstract

Modular top-up unlocks the ‘**Title’ Sponsorship** tier, the highest level of visibility at TOKEN2049. This is an incremental request of  **1,768,167 ada (equivalent to $424,360 USD, calculated at an estimated rate of $0.24 per ada)** to upgrade Cardano’s presence from a 108 sqm booth to a massive 144 sqm footprint and secure a Mainstage Keynote.

**Dependency Notice:** This is a top-up proposal intended to be voted on in conjunction with the Cardano at TOKEN2049 Singapore 2026: Baseline ‘Platinum’ Sponsorship proposal (Gov Action ID: `gov_action18u8lpkzge2csxe3plynn9lh4agwtv3nrqkyfwalwj4ykjv7l68jqqzmul9z`). By splitting the funding request, we enable DReps to make a clear, isolated decision on the value of premium marketing exposure. The baseline ‘Platinum’ proposal ensures Cardano’s presence, while this upgrade proposal asks whether the community wishes to secure additional benefits, including a 15-minute mainstage keynote, premium media access (press release inclusion and introductions), expanded branding via lanyards, a high-traffic engagement point through the protein shake station, and a larger booth footprint, all of which enhance Cardano’s visibility and reach across the event.

The expanded booth and mainstage presence will also amplify the visibility of Cardano ecosystem projects showcasing at the event, providing them with exposure to a broader and more prominent audience.

**Important Notes:** Regarding the vote for this “Cardano at TOKEN2049 Singapore 2026: Top-Up ‘Title’ Sponsorship Upgrade (Modular Extension)”, please vote **"YES"** only if you support the "Cardano at TOKEN2049 Singapore 2026: Baseline ‘Platinum’ Sponsorship Proposal" as a DRep and wish to upgrade it to a ‘Title’ Sponsorship.

### Accountability

EMURGO is committed to fulfilling the project schedule as outlined in the formal legal agreement. The formal acceptance of completed work will be facilitated by a third-party assurer. This entity is tasked with evaluating and approving the deliverables at each project milestone, ensuring they meet the specific criteria set forth in the legal contract. The compensation for this assurance work is drawn from a designated portion of the treasury allocation.

#### Specifics of Budget Management

Intersect will employ a single Treasury Reserve Smart Contract (TRSC) alongside multiple Project-Specific Smart Contracts (PSSC), all under Intersect's administration. The governance structure within Intersect involves three administrative roles and two leadership roles. To ensure accountability, an Oversight Committee composed of five independent, external organizations will implement a system of checks and balances. This committee is designed to prevent unilateral decision-making and mitigate potential errors. While Intersect handles the day-to-day administration of both the TRSC and PSSCs, the Oversight Committee provides crucial external review for specific, high-impact actions.

The 2025 TRSC Oversight Committee includes Sundae Labs, the Cardano Foundation, Dquadrant, Xerberus, and NMKR. Their primary function is to objectively validate significant administrative operations using on-chain logic. They ensure that actions are accurate and consistent with established rules, without imposing subjective judgment on governance matters. Comprehensive details regarding Intersect's setup can be found in the [Smart Contract Guide](https://docs.intersectmbo.org/intersect-knowledge-base/archive/funding-administration-archive/intersect-administration-services/smart-contracts-as-part-of-our-administration) available in the knowledge base.

The top-level permissions are outlined below:

* TRSC Fund and PSSC Modify  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins, two of the five trusted entities, and one of the two Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Disperse  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins, three of the five trusted entities, and both Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Pause and Resume  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins and one of the two Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Sweep  
  * Authorization requires at least one of the three Intersect admins and one of the two Intersect leadership members to sign off.  
* TRSC Reorganize  
  * Authorization requires at least two of the three Intersect admins and three of the five trusted entities to sign off.

#### Operational Procedures

Once this governance proposal is approved, project funds will be deposited into the TRSC's stake account. Neither the TRSC nor the PSSCs will be staked with a Stake Pool Operator (SPO); both will be delegated to the auto-abstain DRep. The funds will then be held in a UTxO within the TRSC.

When the 2026 TRSC is ready, funds will be migrated over via the disburse function. Once the legal contract is in place, funds will be transferred to a PSSC through the fund action, with all milestones and deliverables recorded in the transaction metadata.

A public dashboard will be made available for the community to audit the TRSC and PSSCs, track key metrics on the withdrawn ADA, and verify all fund movements on-chain.
