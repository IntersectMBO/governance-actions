## Title

Reduce govActionDeposit to increase testnet governance participation

## Abstract

We propose to decrease `govActionDeposit` from 100,000 tada (100000000000 tlovelace) to 1,000 tada (1000000000 tlovelace), on testnets.

## Motivation

The testnet faucet gives users 10,000 tada, by reducing the governance action deposit size (`govActionDeposit`) people are able to submit governance actions with the tada provided by the faucet.

## Rationale

This change will make it much easier for people to submit governance actions on testnets.
